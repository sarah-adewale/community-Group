  • FIRST TIME PARENTS

    • Members who either just had their first babies or are pregnant with their first babies.


  • COVENANT PARENTS

    • Parents with children of varying ages that want to support themselves and learn from their various experiences. Provides opportunities for their children to also network with families of similar values.


  • SINGLE PARENTS

    • Individuals who are almost solely responsible for raising their children as a result of distance/separation from or demise of their spouses.


  • PARENTS OF CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS

    • Parents taking care of children with special needs.


  • WORK FROM HOME MOTHERS

    • Mothers who work from home (this includes stay at home moms).


  • YOUNG MEN'S COMMUNITY

    • For mentoring young single men on life, relationships and marriage.


  • WORKING SINGLES

    • Corporate Professionals that are single who are looking to grow in their carriers whilst also forming relationships leading towards marriage.


  • MORE THAN CONQUERORS

    • Those who have conquered/overcoming a terminal illness. To provide advocacy and support members to get back to their normal lives.


  • HEALTH AND FITNESS

    • Those who have a focus on lifestyle changes to improve their general physical wellbeing. They are focused on diet, exercise, and preventive measures against illness.


  • EXPATS AND OTHER NATIONALS

    • A community of members who are non-Nigerians living in Nigeria to provide support to each other as they navigate living and working in Nigeria.


  • PURPOSEFUL WAITING

    • Members waiting on God for a specific need. The goal is to enable people live full lives whilst waiting on God.


  • SENIOR CITIZENS

    • Members that are over 60years or that have grandchildren. The group will organize themselves for impact initiatives, member support, bible study and prayers.


  • NEW SKILLS

    • Members who want to learn something new within three months e.g. new language, baking, biking, makeup, barbing, etc.


  • PROJECTS

    • Members who would like to complete a specific task within a timeframe. Example, making a short film, writing a book etc. The goal is to build accountability and support groups.


  • GRIEF AND LOSS ASSISTANCE & RECOVERY COMMUNITY (GLARC)

    • For individuals who have experienced the pain of loss and require assistance to regain stability and balance. The Community will help members to move beyond the feelings of helplessness and hopelessness to a life of new possibilities.


  • THE DIASPORA GROUP

    • Community of believers living outside Nigeria but follow the ministry of Pastor Poju Oyemade (The Covenant Nation)


  • NEWLY-WEDS/HONEYMOONERS

    • Newly married couples within the first 24 months of their marriage journey.
